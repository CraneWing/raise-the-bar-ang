About Raise the Bar

This is the second of the Free Code Camp full stack challenges to create an app that looks for nightspots in the user's local area and allows them to add themselves to a list that indicates they are going to a location.

It uses a simple version of the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular, Node). The challenge provides experience to the FCC student in full stack development and managing a third-party API.

The app uses a variety of free and open source resources. They are the following:

  • Fonts: From Font Squirrel. The header font is Bitstream Vera Sans, and the body text is Liberation Serif.
  • Icons: All icons are from Iconic Icons and were originally developed for use with the Ionic framework.
  • Photos: All are from the free image site Unsplash.
  • Search API: The Yelp search API v2.0 is used to provide bar data.
